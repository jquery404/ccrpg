Game Introduction

The Community Currency Role Play (CCRP) is a RPG game, which helps you understand the "community coupons" and create awareness of how resources (especially money) flow out of a cummunity.

CCRP has two rounds and one afterwards discussion, you will need to buy five different items to finsh your round.

Please select a role first and use your arrow keys to control your charactors in the game.
